About The Carpentry Studio

Here at The Shutter Studio our Carpentry and Joinery team boast a vast wealth of woodworking knowledge and experience. Our team consists of a dedicated group of true master craftsmen, that have honed their skills through a lifetime of working within the woodworking industry. The team is able to work with clients to design woodworking solutions that not only look stunning, but are practical and built to last. Our huge investment in a state of the art joinery workshop ensure that our carpentry and joinery is of the highest level of quality. We are able to have complete control over the manufacture of exceptional pieces of woodwork that truly capture our clients exact requirements.

Our workshop is setup to manufacture a wide range of products and we offer a range of solutions for everything from Bespoke Furniture and Wall Panelling to Doors, Alcove storage, Book Cases, Wardrobes, Cabinets, and Radiator covers. We also offer a complete furniture design service, working to capture an exact idea or design that you are trying to capture.

As we make everything in house our designs are limitless – so whether you are looking for something simple and contemporary or for a more detailed and traditional we can hand-build our items to exceed expectations for both private and commercial clients.

Our Process

  • Step 1 → Call us to arrange a site visit
  • Step 2 → We take detailed measurements of your space and talk through the options avaliable
  • Step 3 → We send you a quote taking into account any information and details you've given
  • Step 4 → We produce detailed CAD drawings so you can see how your finished carpentry will look and work out any remaining details with you
  • Step 5 → Your order goes into production at our warehouse
  • Step 6 → When your order is finished we call you to arrange installation
