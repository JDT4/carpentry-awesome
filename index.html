Our Process

Step 1 → Call us to arrange a site visit Step 2 → We take detailed measurements of your space and talk through the options avaliable Step 3 → We send you a quote taking into account any information and details you've given Step 4 → We produce detailed CAD drawings so you can see how your finished carpentry will look and work out any remaining details with you Step 5 → Your order goes into production at our warehouse Step 6 → When your order is finished we call you to arrange installation
